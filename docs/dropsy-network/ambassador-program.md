## Overview

The Dropsy Ambassador Program builds a decentralized layer of real people who understand the protocol, believe in what it is building, and represent it authentically across X and within builder and developer communities. Ambassadors are not promoters paid to post links. They are informed, independent individuals who communicate Dropsy's value through their own voice and credibility. The program rewards quality, consistency, and genuine contribution over volume and noise.

---

## Program Philosophy

- Quality over quantity. Ten ambassadors who deeply understand Dropsy outperform a hundred who just post ticker dropsy.
- Independent by design. Ambassadors post, engage, and represent on their own initiative without waiting for instructions.
- Long-term presence over short-term hype. Consistent voices across months matter more than a burst of activity around a single event.
- Speak to builders. Dropsy's target audience is developers and project teams. Ambassador content should reach and resonate with that crowd.
- Authentic voice always. Posts should sound like the ambassador, not like a copied announcement.

---

## Ambassador Tiers

The program runs on three tiers. As you move up, the grind decreases and the influence increases. Higher tiers do less execution work and more strategic work. The reward at the top is proximity to decisions, better allocation, and reduced obligations.

---

### TIER 1 | CONTRIBUTOR

#### Who This Is

New ambassadors who proved their intent through action. They’ve demonstrated commitment and consistency during the simulation phase, earning their place in the program.

This is the **execution tier** where consistency matters more than scale.

#### Core Responsibilities

- Create: Minimum of 2 original posts per week about Dropsy on X
- Amplify: Actively engage with and amplify Dropsy content (likes, replies, reposts)
- Align: Stay consistent with Dropsy narrative and messaging and posting schedule

#### Access and Rewards

- Access to the ambassador-only channel
- Contributor role in Discord
- Base amplification within Dropsy Ecosystem
- Base eligibility to the ambassador allocation Pool
- Early access to Dropsy updates before public announcements

### TIER 2 | ADVOCATE

#### Who This Is

Ambassadors who have completed at least 6 weeks in Tier 1 and demonstrated a clear understanding of Dropsy’s architecture and narrative.
This tier marks the shift from execution → quality.

#### Core Responsibilities

- **Create :** At least one deeper post per week: a thread, an explainer, or a reaction to a Solana or Dropsy development.

- **Amplify with intent** (meaningful replies, discussions, positioning, not passive engagement).

- **Guide Tier 1 contributors** through support and example.

- **Host or participate in 1 AMA / session per month** (sharing knowledge, strategies, or feedback)

#### Access and Rewards

- **Advocate** role with elevated Discord permissions

- **Priority amplification** within the Dropsy ecosystem (higher visibility on selected posts and narratives)

- **Early eligibility for the on-chain referral system** (access before public launch with first-mover advantage)

- **Priority allocation** from the Ambassador allocation pool (higher than Tier 1)

- **Direct line to the community lead** for coordination, suggestions and feedback

### TIER 3 | SENATE

#### Who This Is

Top-performing ambassadors who have consistently delivered high-quality contributions and demonstrated exceptional leadership, reliability, and strategic thinking. selected by invitation only to become shift from contributors to operators and decision shapers within the Dropsy ecosystem.

#### Core Responsibilities

- Lead initiatives : (own campaigns, content directions, or growth experiments from idea to execution)
- Set the standard and Coordinate amplification publish high-signal content that defines narrative and positioning with direct Tier 1 & Tier 2 efforts on key moments.
- Mentor Tier 2 & Tier 1 develop talent, not just support.
- Drive BD conversations by connecting Dropsy to projects and developers who could become delegate partners.
- Represent Dropsy in inter-community events, X Spaces, and ecosystem discussions
- Provide strategic feedback contribute ideas, identify opportunities, and surface ecosystem insights

#### Access and Rewards

- Senate role with the highest community status in the program.
- Top-tier amplification priority content is consistently positioned and distributed across the ecosystem.
- Highest allocation tier from the ambassador Pool.
- Direct access to core team / founders.
- Early & extended access to the on-chain referral system.
- Involvement in internal discussions early input on strategy, campaigns, and feature direction

## Evaluation System

Each Tier functions as a collective unit with shared standards and evaluation.

Ambassadors operate within a **tier-based structure**, where each tier represents both a level of responsibility and a collective performance group.

Each tier is evaluated not only individually, but also as a **shared ecosystem of contributors working under the same standard**.

Performance is measured through a monthly human voting system with two layers:

### Layer 1: Peer Vote

Ambassadors within the same tier evaluate each other monthly.

▸ Vote for the most impactful contributors in their tier.
▸ Criteria: engagement quality, consistency, supportiveness.
▸ Conducted via Discord Poll (48h window).

### Layer 2: Senate & Core Team Evaluation

Separately, Tier 3 Senate members and the core team vote on standout ambassadors across all Tiers.

- Focus: external impact on the Dropsy ecosystem
- Criteria: Discipline & Commitment, Content-quality, Narrative contribution to Dropsy, Influence on builders and communities, Collaborations & Network Activation

## Content Standards

Because Dropsy's real audience is developers and project teams who might build with its infrastructure, ambassador content should speak to that crowd, not just to airdrop hunters

### What Good Ambassador Content Looks Like

- Written in your own voice, not copied from an announcement or generated entirely by AI
- Speaks to builders: explains why Dropsy's infrastructure matters to developers and anyone thinking about token distribution
- Specific: references actual features, real mechanisms, or genuine personal experience with the product
- Timely: reacts to relevant Solana ecosystem news or Dropsy updates while they are still fresh
- Honest: no price speculation, no promises of guaranteed returns or allocations

### What Will Not Be Approved

- Generic posts with no substance: 'Check out Dropsy Protocol, great project'
- Copy-pasted announcements with no personal commentary
- Price speculation or anything that could be read as financial advice
- Posts that misstate what Dropsy is or how it works
- Duplicate content submitted more than once in any form

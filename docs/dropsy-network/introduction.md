## What is Dropsy Network

The Dropsy Network is the engine behind growth, visibility, and adoption.

It is not just a community, it is a structured system of contributors, creators, and operators working together to expand Dropsy’s reach and narrative.

The Dropsy network is composed of individuals who actively contribute to Dropsy through:

- Content creation
- Storytelling and narrative building
- Community engagement
- Strategic amplification (raids, campaigns, collaborations)

Every action within the network is designed to create momentum.

---

## Core Pillars

### Ambassador Program

Ambassadors are long-term contributors focused on quality and influence.

They:

- Create high-signal content
- Educate and onboard new users
- Shape the narrative around Dropsy
- Collaborate with other builders and communities

This is a role built on **consistency, discipline, and impact**.

---

### Raid Program

Raiders focus on distribution and visibility.

They:

- Amplify key messages and announcements
- Support campaigns through coordinated engagement
- Increase reach across platforms
- Help content break into wider audiences

Raiding is not spam it is **targeted, meaningful engagement at scale**.

---

## A Structured System

The Dropsy Network operates through a tiered system.

Each tier reflects:

- Level of contribution
- Quality of work
- Consistency over time
- Impact on the ecosystem

Progression is earned through performance — not just activity.

---

## Why it Matters

Great products need strong narratives and distribution.

The Dropsy Network ensures that:

- Ideas are seen
- Content reaches the right audience
- Momentum is sustained over time

This is how Dropsy grows not just as a product, but as an ecosystem.

## What is Dropsy ?

**Dropsy is the token distribution layer for Solana, enabling creators, protocols, and teams to distribute tokens with zero friction.**

With Airdrop Master, teams can create their own airdrop systems, customize fee models, and even build entire token-distribution products on top of Dropsy.

---

## Why Dropsy Exists ?

Token distribution is still messy. Every team rebuilds the same logic, wastes time on security, and struggles with scalable claim systems.

**Dropsy eliminates this overhead** by providing a unified, on-chain, trustless distribution layer.

---

## Who Is Dropsy For ?

Dropsy is built for:

- Protocol teams launching tokens
- NFT projects rewarding holders
- dApps needing airdrop logic
- Creators distributing community rewards
- Developers building token-launch platforms
- DAOs running incentive programs

## 🧩 What Are DAO Bears?

DAO Bears form the **core governance and identity layer** of the entire Dropsy ecosystem. They serve two fundamental purposes:

---

### 🎁 **1. Designed to Reward**

DAO Bears are a recognition of early alignment with Dropsy. They are distributed to:

- **Early supporters** who believed in the project from the beginning
- **Whitelisted community members** who actively contributed
- **Mint Pass holders**, each receiving **one free DAO Bear mint** (snapshot details announced separately)

These rewards reinforce Dropsy’s commitment to giving value back to its earliest ecosystem contributors.

---

### 🧱 **2. Designed to Represent the Community**

Each DAO Bear symbolizes one of the foundational archetypes of the Dropsy ecosystem:

- **Builders**
- **Creators**
- **Developers**
- **Community members**

Together, the collection expresses the culture, values, and long-term vision of Dropsy.

---

DAO Bears play a crucial role in **governance**, **protocol direction**, and **ecosystem ownership**, making them a central pillar of the Dropsy future.

---

## 🧠 Utility

### **1. DAO Governance Power**

Each DAO Bear represents governance weight in future Dropsy DAO decisions:

- Protocol upgrades
- Fee structure changes
- Treasury usage
- Ecosystem expansions

### **2. Hybrid MPL Token Swapping**

DAO Bears are built using **MPL Hybrid Mechanics**, meaning:

- Each DAO Bear can be **swapped into 12500 [$Dropsy](/dropsy-token/overview) tokens**
- Holders can later swap **$Dropsy back into a DAO Bear** (if supply remains)
- This ensures long‑term value, liquidity, and flexibility

### **3. Rewards & Holder Benefits**

Active holders receive:

- Boosts in future airdrop mechanics
- Early eligibility for partnerships and collaborations
- Priority access during future protocol expansions

### **4. Ecosystem Access**

DAO Bear holders unlock:

- Private DAO discussion channels
- Access to governance meetings
- IRL & virtual event priority

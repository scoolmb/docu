## 🔥 Utilities

Mint Pass holders unlock a focused set of **core utilities**, each built to reward early supporters and provide real value across the ecosystem.

### • Farming Boosters

**Why?** Holding a Mint Pass grants access to **higher farming boosters**, which scale based on:

- Your **Discord role tiers**, and
- Your **NFT holding count** Mint Pass

This creates a dynamic reward system where active and committed community members earn more.

### • Zealy Points Boost

**Why?** Since Dropsy uses Zealy for quests, Mint Pass holders will consistently receive:

- **Extra Zealy XP** through quests specifically made for holders
- **Boosted farming benefits**, because Zealy points affect farming weight

### • Free Mint: Dropsy DAO Bears

**Why?** A Mint Pass automatically secures a **free whitelist spot** to mint a **Dropsy DAO Bear**.

- Mint Pass holders mint **1 Bear for free**
- Snapshot requirements will be announced ahead of time

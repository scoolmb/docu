# 🌾 $DROPSY Token Farming

Dropsy Token Farming is the first phase of the reward program designed to distribute future $DROPSY tokens to active community members.

**The token is not live yet**, so **Season 1** farming uses **Dropsy Diamonds** as the temporary reward currency.

Diamonds represent your **future share of the $DROPSY token allocation** once the token launches.

---

## 🎯 Goals of Farming

- Reward real, active community members
- Build early engagement before token launch
- Distribute future tokens to people who contribute
- Grow awareness of Dropsy across Web3
- Prepare the ecosystem for Next Season farming

---

## 💎 What Are Dropsy Diamonds?

**Dropsy Diamonds = Farming Points.**  
They are **non-transferable**, **off-chain**, and act as a **placeholder for your future $DROPSY rewards**.

When the token is launched:

- Your Diamonds will be converted to a proportional allocation of **$DROPSY**.
- No new tokens are minted, rewards come from the ecosystem allocation.

---

## 💠 How to Farm Diamonds

You can earn Diamonds through multiple channels during Season 1:

### **1. Zealy Sprint Participation**

Join Dropsy’s Zealy sprints and complete quests to earn Diamonds.

### **2. Off-chain Referrals**

Invite new friends to Dropsy using your referral link.  
Each successful referral grants Diamonds.

### **3. Discord Games**

Participate in fun community games on Discord and earn Diamonds based on your performance.

🗓 Weekly Games Schedule and Rewards

- **🎨 [Gartic](#-gartic) — Mondays at 5 PM UTC**

- **🤼 [Royal Rumble](#-royal-rumble) — Wednesdays at 5 PM UTC**

- **🧠 [Trivia Night](#-trivia-night) — Saturdays at 7 PM UTC**

---

### **4. Ecosystem Engagement (More Coming)**

Future activities may include:

- Using Dropsy tools

- Social engagement waves

Each farming path has its own multiplier and reward rules.

## **Farming Boost Tiers**

Farming Boost Tiers reward active community members with higher Diamond earnings. Each tier requires both the minimum [Mint Pass NFTs](/dropsy-nfts/mint-pass) and the corresponding Discord role in the same path, holding only one is not enough. Higher tiers provide bigger boosts, helping users maximize their future $DROPSY token allocation.

| **Tier**       | **Diamond Boost** | **Minimum NFT Requirement** | **Minimum Discord Role** |
| -------------- | ----------------- | --------------------------- | ------------------------ |
| **Starter**    | 10%               | 0 NFTs                      | Verified Role            |
| **Early**      | 20%               | 0 NFTs                      | Verified Role            |
| **OG**         | 50%               | 0 NFTs                      | Verified Role            |
| **Ambassador** | 75%               | 1 NFT                       | Early-Bird Role          |
| **Elite**      | 100%              | 2 NFTs                      | OG Role                  |

## 🔮 What Happens After Season 1?

Once the $DROPSY token is launched:

- Diamonds will convert into your token allocation.
- Season 2 farming will begin with on-chain reward emissions.
- Early participants gain a stronger position in future seasons.

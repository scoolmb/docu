# 🪙 $Dropsy Token

## Overview

More than just a token, **$DROPSY forms the foundation of a scalable distribution layer on Solana**.  
It aligns incentives between **creators**, **airdrop participants**, **community members**, **NFT holders**, **affiliates**, and the **protocol** itself.

---

## 📌 Why $DROPSY Exists ?

At the heart of Dropsy, $DROPSY drives alignment and rewards the entire ecosystem, including:

- **Loyal community members**
- **Contributors**
- **Creators**
- **Partners**
- **Affiliates**

This creates a **self-reinforcing loop** between distribution, participation, contribution, and long-term growth.

# ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

This page covers the most common questions about the Dropsy Protocol, the $DROPSY token, airdrops, vesting, and technical integration.

---

# 🧩 **General Questions**

## **What is Dropsy?**

Dropsy is the **token distribution layer for Solana**, enabling secure, scalable, and customizable airdrops for creators, protocols, and communities.

---

## **Who can use Dropsy?**

Anyone:

- Project founders
- NFT collections
- Developers
- DAOs
- Token creators

If you need to distribute tokens Dropsy is the right Choice.

---

## **Is Dropsy open-source?**

The SDK and documentation are fully open-source, so anyone can review, audit, or integrate with Dropsy.

The smart contract program itself is not open-source at this time, though it will be fully auditable and tested.

---

# 🪙 **$DROPSY Token**

## **What is the $DROPSY token used for?**

- Fee discounts
- Boosting visibility of airdrops
- Affiliate level upgrades
- Ecosystem priority access
- Governance
- Staking (not guaranteed)

---

## **Will the token have staking?**

Staking is not currently planned or guaranteed.
If implemented in the future, it could potentially be used to:

- Reduce platform fees for creators and users

- Boost farming points in the ecosystem

- Gain priority access to upcoming features and programs

---

## **Is there a fixed supply?**

Yes. Total supply is **1,000,000,000 $DROPSY**.

---

# 🎁 **Airdrops & Claims**

## **How do airdrops work on Dropsy?**

Dropsy distributes tokens using:

- Merkle proofs
- Bitmap accounts
- Optional vesting schedules (not yet available)

This ensures claims are **cheap, secure, and scalable**.

---

## **Can creators choose whether an airdrop is vested?**

Yes, creators can configure:

- No vesting
- Linear vesting
- Step-wise vesting
- Curved vesting

---

## **Why is my claim not working?**

Common reasons:

- The airdrop has ended
- You’re not included in the merkle list (not eligible)
- Your wallet already claimed
- Your wallet may not have enough SOL to cover transaction fees

Check the airdrop page or contact the creator.

---

# 🛠 **Developer Questions**

## **Is there an SDK?**

Yes — TypeScript SDK is provided for:

- Creating airdrops
- Managing vesting (not yet available)
- Claiming tokens
- Writing integrations

---

## **How are recipients tracked?**

Dropsy uses:

- Airdrop account → global config
- Bitmap accounts → claim status
- Merkle proofs → eligibility

Result: scalable to **hundreds of thousands** of recipients.

---

## **Can I use Dropsy sdk to Build DApps**

Yes. Dropsy sdk is **open-source**, and devs can integrate it in their own dapps.
